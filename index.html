Frequently Asked Questions

This section contains a list of frequently asked questions regarding Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us.
Will food and drinks be available for purchase? Can I bring my own food? Why is there a bag check now? When should I come? How much are tickets? Where do I park? Can I bring my service animal?
Yes! This year, we're partnering with DeKalb Market Hall to bring in some of Brooklyn's favorite food vendors. We'll have kebabs, sandwiches, popcorn, cake pops, and so much more! To go along with these treats, beer and wine will be available for purchase, as well.
Of course -- feel free to bring in your own food, but outside alcohol is still prohibited on the lawn. To that end, be advised that bag checks will be conducted at the entrance.
Outside alcohol and glass bottles have always been prohibited in the Park. This year, we're partnering with DeKalb Market Hall to bring food, beer, and wine vending to the festival. With these new offerings, we want to remind you of this policy in order to ensure the safety and security of festival goers. We hope you enjoy these new options!
Food will start serving at 5:00 PM, and movies begin at sundown (6:00 PM). Make sure to come at least 30 minutes before the screening to move through bag check, stake out a good spot, and take in the spectacular skyline!
The event is free of charge and open to everyone of all ages! We only ask that you fill out the pre-registration form to let us know you are coming!
Parking near Brooklyn Bridge Park is very limited. We highly recommend taking public transporation, biking or walking to the park. BBP is accessible by bus, subway and ferry.
Service animals are welcome!
